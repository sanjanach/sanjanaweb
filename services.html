Services

Waste Collection

GDSS collects waste from several types of containers, including drums, skips (2 and 4 cuyd), roll-off containers (10, 20, 30 and 40 cuyd) and compactors (15 and 30cuyd). We will assess your business operations to determine the best equipment to suit your waste management requirements.

We provide dependable waste collection, and our professional staff is trained and standing by to answer all of your service-related questions.
We have also expanded the scope of our operations and now provide comprehensive waste management solutions, including recycling facilities and special waste disposal services.

Consultations & Assessments

With more than 100 years of combined experience, our professional team includes an environmental and solid waste specialists who will provide invaluable consulting services to help your company attain or maintain any environmental standards or certification.

  • Sanitation consultations, including Waste Audits, Waste Assessments and Waste Reduction Programmes
  • Development and implementation assistance for Environmental Management Systems
  • Training and certification in relation to Occupational Health & Safety
  • Training in issues related to waste and environmental management
  • Health, Safety & Risk Assessments

Special Wastes

GDSS recognises that our customers will generate wastes which, for whatever reason, cannot handled like normal waste.
